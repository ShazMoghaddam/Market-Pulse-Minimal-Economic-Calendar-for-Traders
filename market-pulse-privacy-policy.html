Market Pulse

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 April 2026  ·  Developer: Shaz Moghaddam

📋 Short version: Market Pulse does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app works entirely on your device and only makes network requests to fetch public economic calendar data.

1. Who We Are

Market Pulse is developed and published by Shaz Moghaddam ("we", "us", "our"). If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at the address listed at the end of this document.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. The app does not require you to create an account, provide your name, email address, or any other personal details.

The only data stored by the app is saved locally on your device using your browser's localStorage mechanism. This includes:

  • Your selected dark/light theme preference
  • Your selected region and impact filter preferences
  • A temporary cache of economic calendar event data (to allow offline use)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

3. Third-Party API Calls

If you configure the app with a TradingEconomics API key, the app will make outbound HTTPS requests to api.tradingeconomics.com to retrieve live economic calendar data. These requests are made directly from your device to TradingEconomics' servers. We do not intercept, log, or store these requests.

Please refer to the TradingEconomics Privacy Policy for information on how they handle data from API calls.

Without a configured API key, all data is served from static fallback data bundled inside the app. No network requests are made in this mode.

4. Google Fonts

The app loads the Inter and JetBrains Mono fonts from Google Fonts when you have an active internet connection. This means your device makes a request to Google's font servers. Please refer to the Google Privacy Policy for details.

5. Analytics and Tracking

We do not use any analytics services, tracking pixels, cookies, advertising SDKs, or any form of behavioural tracking. We have no visibility into how you use the app.

6. Children's Privacy

Market Pulse is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. The app displays financial data intended for adult traders and investors.

7. Data Security

All data stored by the app remains on your device under the security controls of your operating system and browser engine. We do not operate any servers that store user data, so there is no risk of a server-side data breach affecting you.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.
